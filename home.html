Welcome to the Wonderful World of SAORI Weaving.

We are so glad you dropped by!

BEAUTIFULcloth is a registered SAORI Studio, run by authorised SAORI instructor, Amanda I’Anson and established since 2017. We are delighted to be based at our new premises at 18 Sheaf St, Daventry, Northamptonshire. We offer workshops and classes in SAORI freeform weaving for individuals and groups of all ages, abilities and levels of experience. Our aim is to encourage everyone to explore their own creativity through art weaving by providing an encouraging and welcoming environment, practical support and wonderful easy-to-use looms and equipment. We are also an authorised retailer of SAORI looms and equipment. Please take your time to have a wander around and if you need any further information, or just want to say hello, please drop us a line by clicking here.
